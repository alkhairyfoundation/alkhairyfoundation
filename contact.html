Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can enroll your child by contacting our Programs Department at foundationalkhairy@gmail.com or calling +234 802 353 2870, +234 703 627 8580, or +2349033460322. We'll guide you through the enrollment process, which typically includes an initial assessment, program orientation, and registration. Most programs have specific intake periods, so we recommend contacting us at least one month before the program start date.

Many of our core programs are offered free of charge or at highly subsidized rates to ensure accessibility. Some specialized programs or materials may have associated costs, but we offer scholarships and financial assistance for families in need. Our goal is to never turn away a student due to financial constraints. Contact us to discuss specific program fees and available support options.

We welcome volunteers in various capacities: as tutors, mentors, event organizers, administrative support, and more. To volunteer, please email foundationalkhairy@gmail.com with your areas of interest, skills, and availability. We'll schedule an orientation session and match you with suitable opportunities. All volunteers undergo a screening process and receive training specific to their role.

Yes! We partner with schools, mosques, businesses, and other organizations. Partnership opportunities include program implementation, resource sharing, sponsorship, and collaborative events. Please contact our Partnerships Department at foundationalkhairy@gmail.com with details about your organization and proposed collaboration. We'll schedule a meeting to explore mutually beneficial partnership models.

You can donate through bank transfer, online payment, or in-person at our events. Visit our Donate page for detailed information on donation methods. We accept one-time donations, monthly recurring donations, and program-specific sponsorships. All donations are tax-deductible, and we provide official receipts for your records. For large donations or specific project funding, please contact foundationalkhairy@gmail.com.

While our headquarters is in Ibadan, we work with partner organizations and schools across Nigeria and plan to expand to other African countries. We also offer some online programs accessible from anywhere. If you're interested in bringing our programs to your community, please contact our Partnerships Department to discuss expansion opportunities, training for local facilitators, or establishing affiliate programs.
