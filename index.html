Our Story

From 90 students to a movement transforming African Muslim youth

It Started With 90 Students

In Ibadan, we sat with 90 public school students. We listened to their dreams. We documented their ambitions. We saw the potential that the world was missing.

What We Discovered: These teenagers want to become doctors, engineers, teachers, entrepreneurs, and leaders. They have the intelligence. They have the drive. What they lack is mentorship, guidance, hope, and a clear path forward.

We're building that path. The opportunity is clear: Africa has the world's youngest and fastest-growing population. If we invest in Muslim youth now—with proper mentorship, Islamic character, academic support, and skills training—they will become the leaders who transform the continent and uplift the Ummah globally.
