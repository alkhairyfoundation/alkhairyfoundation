BEYOND THE HORIZON

Nurturing the Next Generation of Leaders

May 1 & 3, 2026 Mokola, Ibadan
Register Now View Schedule

Nurturing Excellence

MPC 2026 is a cornerstone event of the Alkhairy Foundation, designed to bridge the gap between today's challenges and tomorrow's leadership. Set against the theme "Beyond the Horizon," this conference focuses on the holistic development of Muslim youth and the critical role of parental guidance.

Our mission is to empower the next generation with the ethical foundation, academic rigor, and leadership skills necessary to navigate a complex world while remaining firmly rooted in their faith.

Conference Goals: To inspire vision, build character, and foster a supportive community of learners and mentors.
