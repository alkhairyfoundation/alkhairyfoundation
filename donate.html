Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about donations

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Al-Khairy Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible. You will receive an official receipt that can be used for tax purposes.

How is my donation used?

100% of your donation goes directly to our programs. We maintain 0% administrative fees. Your contribution funds student scholarships, educational materials, Islamic programs, and skills training workshops.

Can I sponsor a specific student?

Yes! We offer a student sponsorship program where you can directly support a specific student. You'll receive regular updates on their progress and can establish a mentorship relationship if desired.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and mobile payments. All online payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Absolutely! We offer monthly, quarterly, and yearly recurring donation options. Recurring donations provide consistent support for our programs and help us plan more effectively.

How do I get a donation receipt?

For online donations, you'll receive an automatic receipt via email. For bank transfers, email your transfer details to foundationalkhairy@gmail.com and we'll send you a receipt within 48 hours.
